The Future of Data

Unify your knowledge and data in Unize. Everything is a page with a template (like "person"), properties (like "age"), and relationships (like "friends with") with other pages.

The Future of Apps

Most apps only work for particular types of data, like customers (CRMs) and restaurants (Yelp). Replace them with Unize's general-use data views and automations.

The Future of the Web

Most of the internet is undiscoverable, low-quality text. Knowledge in Web 10 is organized by default (with help from AI) and quality checked (with help from crowdsourcing).

The Future of AI

AI is a long way from being safe and useful. Unize AI is intertwined with Web 10 for knowledge and reasoning, enabling it to reliably perform knowledge work for users.
